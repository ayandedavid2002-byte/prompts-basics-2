##    Objective: Convert vague requests into precise prompts.
###    Start with a vague request like: “Tell me about AI.”
####    Prompt 
-   Tell me about Data Science
####    Result 
**Here’s a broad overview of data science:**
Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to extract insights and knowledge from structured and unstructured data. It combines elements of statistics, computer science, and domain expertise to analyze data, identify patterns, and support decision-making. Data scientists work with data collection, cleaning, modeling, and visualization to solve complex problems, make predictions, and optimize processes across industries like finance, healthcare, marketing, and technology.
###   Refine it step by step into a clear instruction, e.g. 1.“Explain AI in simple terms for beginners”. 2. “Summarize the main AI techniques in 5 bullet points.”
####    Prompt 1
-   Explain Data Science in simple terms for beginners
####    Result 
**Here’s a simple explanation of data science for beginners:**
Data science is all about using data to answer questions and solve problems. Imagine you have a lot of information, numbers, facts, or records and you want to understand what it means or predict what might happen next. Data scientists collect the data, clean it, look for patterns, and use computer programs or math to make sense of it so people or companies can make better decisions.
**In short:** data science turns raw information into useful knowledge.
####    Prompt 2
-   Summarize the main Data Science techniques in 5 bullet points.
####    Result 
**Here are 5 main data science techniques summarized in bullet points:**
- **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing:** Removing errors, handling missing values, and preparing data for analysis.
- **Statistical Analysis:** Using statistics to find patterns, correlations, and trends in data.
- **Machine Learning:** Creating models that can learn from data to make predictions or decisions.
- **Data Visualization:** Representing data visually through charts, graphs, or dashboards to make insights easier to understand.
- **Big Data Analytics:** Processing and analyzing large, complex datasets using specialized tools and technologies.
###    Compare outputs and note differences.
- The three outputs differ in terms of audience, tone, and focus: **“Tell me about data science”** provides a broad and formal overview of the Data Science field, **“Explain data science in simple terms for beginners”** simplifies the concept using plain language for easy understanding, and **“Summarize the main data science techniques in 5 bullet points”** is structured, concise, and method-focused, highlighting specific techniques rather than general concepts.
